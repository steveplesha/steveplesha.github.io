School Emergency Form

This is a project built for the school I work at as a support tech. The security officer found another district that used a Google form for teachers to let school administration know if there was an emergency in their room. The idea was to make the reporting functionality available if the teacher was not able to get to speak on the phone. The data gets sent to a Google spreadsheet and an email is sent to select staff when a response is submitted. The problem was that the form wasn't too functional on mobile devices. So I took the form and made it mobile-ready while still sending the data back to the Google Doc and shooting off the email. I also had the form match the color scheme on the corporation website.
